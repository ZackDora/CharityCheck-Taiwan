Mico's Animal Care Association
咪可思關懷流浪動物協會

Introduction

Since its establishment, the association has successfully rescued and adopted out nearly a thousand stray cats, helping them find loving homes. This achievement not only provides shelter for these animals but also raises public awareness about the plight of strays

The association relies on a dedicated team of volunteers who provide daily care for stray cats and participate in community outreach activities. Their efforts promote awareness of animal protection issues and encourage more people to engage in adoption and volunteer services, fostering a sense of community involvement

Types of Charity

  • Transitional Home

    • Aims to provide proper care for homeless cats and help them find permanent homes. In addition to caring for cats at the shelter, the organization also rescues animals from shelters to increase their visibility and chances of adoption.

    The association has taken in nearly a hundred stray cats, including those that are old, sick, or disabled. They provide regular veterinary check-ups, vaccinations, medical care, and nutrition. Despite the high costs associated with caring for long-term residents , Mico's has successfully facilitated the adoption of nearly a thousand cats since 2017, thanks to a strict and reliable adoption process that minimizes returns.

  • Journal

    • The journal aims to promote awareness and knowledge about the care of stray animals, particularly focusing on stray cats. It covers topics such as their care, health management, and various pet-related themes. Each issue includes practical recipes, medical care information, introductions to common diseases, and event reports. The goal is to increase public attention on stray animals and encourage adoption and volunteer participation.

  • Volunteer

    • Provide care and support for homeless cats and participate in various activities and projects organized by the association. Their responsibilities include daily feeding, cleaning, and medical care, as well as assisting with adoption events to help stray animals find loving homes.

    The association regularly conducts training sessions for volunteers to ensure they acquire essential knowledge and skills, such as health management and care techniques for stray animals. Additionally, volunteers engage in community outreach activities to raise awareness about the importance of protecting stray animals and encourage public involvement in adoption and volunteer services. These efforts not only enhance public awareness but also increase adoption rates and volunteer participation.

Donation Usages

  • Stray Animals' Daily Fee

    The Mikos Animal Care Association has a program called “Cat Rescue and Adoption”. They usually rescue stray cats, provide them with medical care, and facilitate their adoption into loving homes. The location of this activity is primarily in Taoyuan, Taiwan, where they operate various shelters and outreach programs.

  • Educational Outreach

    Mikos actively conducts educational programs, particularly in schools and communities, to promote knowledge about stray animal protection. These initiatives help instill proper animal welfare values in children, laying a foundation for future adoptions and care for strays

  • Raising Social Awareness

    Through collaboration with local governments and organizations, the association advocates for improved regulations and policies regarding animal protection, enhancing societal awareness of animal stray issues
