Introduction

China Long-Term Care Association provides the elderly with a variety of services. Ranging from basic daily care to tending the invalid, it also raises our awareness about the phenomenon of the aging population and its underlying problems in Taiwan. Most importantly, it prevents lonely death among the aged by dispatching caretakers so as to enhance their living qualities.

Types of Charity

  • Caring Plans and Services for Three Stages of Elderly

    • For the Healthy: Routine visits, meals offering, escort, and formulation of plans facilitating health

    For the Sub Healthy: Doing chores (the home care system), facilitating health both physically and mentally, preventive medicine, and daily care

    For the Losing Health: Body care (the home care system), medical treatment, and tracking health

    At the Point of Death: Palliative care, spiritual care, and funeral management
