Taipei Orphan Welfare
失親兒福利基金會

Introduction

Founded in 1968, and transitioned to a foundation in July 2007. The Orphan Welfare Foundation collaborates with other enterprises as well to assist needy children, espcially those whose parents are deprived of their abilities to tend to their kids because of illness, diseases, accidents, and so on. For the past eight years, it has helped 13800 students, and 2000 of them smoothly finished their studies.

Types of Charity

  • Schooling

    • Home visiting, scholarships/fellowships, and studies and higher education counselling.

  • Family

    • Psychotherapy and financial assistance

  • Searching Careers

    • Camping, vocational training, and double-tracked plans
